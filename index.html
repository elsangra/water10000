    SUSTAINABILITY AND MANAGEMENT OF 10,000 WATER PROJECTS

    The state department of water and sanitation has put up some measures to sustain and manage the 10,000 water projects
    through water and sanitation services as a
    shared responsibilities, management of water for school boreholes and no-revenue water management as follows:-

    WATER AND SANITATION SERVICES

    The sustainable water and sanitation services provision is a shared responsibility between national and county governments as per the constitution of Kenya.The aim is to ensure the right of every person to clean and safe water in adequate quantities by developing sustainable water systems.This also includes the development of policies, and regulations and also owning, protecting, and sharing natural resources.

    MANAGEMENT OF WATER FOR SCHOOL BOREHOLES

    Boreholes are strategically located in public institutions and will also serve nearby communities.There is an equitable distribution of boreholes across the wards to increase impacts.Also it establish a committee led by school administration and comprise of representatives from community members and benefitting institutions.It helps the management committee to develop a business plan and recommend affordable tariffs to assist in the management of the infrastructure.

    NON-REVENUE WATER MANAGEMENT

    Through the MOUs signed between Ministry and County Governments, there is a framework for the management of Non-Revenue Water to improve the current water and sanitation access.In addition, each county is expected to develop a roadmap and implementation plan to guide the reduction of NRW with clear annual performance indicators.The process should ensure stakeholders and public participation as guided by the constitution and relevant laws.

    Our Purpose

    To ensure water resources availability and accessibility by all. To contribute to national development by promoting and supporting integrated water resource management to enhance water availability and accessibility. All of our aim can conquer the following;

    Reducing Lining of Water Collection

    Through the availability of water within the society can lead also to the reduction on lining during the collection and fetching of water.

    Ensuring Water Availability

    The Ministry's mandate entails the development and management of Water Resources, drainage and land reclamation, Sanitation and Irrigation. This includes water harvesting and storage in order to reduce pressure on freshwater resources; and make available adequate water for supply and usage.

    Reduce Poverty in the Society

    But the contribution of water management to poverty reduction goes far beyond just drinking water and sanitation: water is essential for improving the health and agriculture development which can lower poverty rate in the society.
